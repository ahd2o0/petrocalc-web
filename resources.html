Resources & References

Here you can find useful resources, recommended books, and key references related to petroleum engineering.

Recommended Books

These books are widely recognized as fundamental texts in various fields of petroleum engineering. You can typically find them through major academic publishers or online booksellers.

  • "Applied Petroleum Reservoir Engineering" by B.C. Craft, M. Hawkins, and R.E. Terry
    A cornerstone for reservoir engineering students and professionals, covering fundamentals and practical applications.
  • "Petroleum Production Engineering" by Boyun Guo and William C. Lyons
    Comprehensive coverage of petroleum production systems, from wellbore to surface facilities.
  • "Drilling Engineering" by J.J. Azar and G. Robello Samuel
    An essential reference for drilling operations, well planning, and design.
  • "Well Logging for Earth Scientists" by Darwin V. Ellis and Julian M. Singer
    A detailed guide to the principles and applications of well logging techniques.

Where to find these books:
Most of these titles are available from academic publishers like Wiley, Elsevier, or Springer. You can also search for them on major online bookselling platforms such as Google Books or Amazon.
