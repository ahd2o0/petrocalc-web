Techlog Tutorials

Techlog Tutorials

Techlog is a comprehensive wellbore-centric software platform used for petrophysical evaluation, rock physics, and data integration. It's crucial for understanding subsurface properties from well log data.

To find more tutorials: Search YouTube for "Techlog tutorial" or "Techlog petrophysics."

Pipesim Tutorials

Pipesim Tutorials

Pipesim (Schlumberger) is a steady-state multiphase flow simulator used for designing and optimizing production systems. It helps engineers understand fluid behavior in wells and pipelines and predict system performance.

To find more tutorials: Search YouTube for "Pipesim tutorial" or "Pipesim simulation."

PVTi Tutorials

PVTi Tutorials

PVTi (Schlumberger) is a PVT (Pressure-Volume-Temperature) simulation software used to model the phase behavior of reservoir fluids. It's crucial for accurately predicting how oil and gas will behave under varying reservoir and surface conditions.

To find more tutorials: Search YouTube for "PVTi tutorial" or "PVTi fluid modeling."

Excel for Petroleum Engineering

Excel for Petroleum Engineering

Microsoft Excel is an indispensable tool in petroleum engineering for data analysis, calculations, modeling, and visualization. These tutorials cover its applications in various oil and gas workflows.

To find more tutorials: Search YouTube for "Excel petroleum engineering" or "Excel oil and gas calculations."
